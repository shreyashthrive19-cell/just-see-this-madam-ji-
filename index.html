A little something from my heart 💌

Hi Vanshu,

Bas kuch cheezein calmly bolni thi. Lagta hai hum dono hi aise phase me the jahan cheezein properly handle nahi ho paayi. Shayad tum apni side se overwhelmed thi aur main apni side se utna clearly express nahi kar paaya jitna mujhe karna chahiye tha.

Main tumse milna isliye chahata tha kyunki mujhe chats pe apni feelings achhe se express karna thoda difficult lagta hai. Mujhe laga milke calmly baat karenge toh hum ek dusre ko better samajh paayenge.

Aur jab tumne bola tha ki tumhe space chahiye, toh maine socha shayad tumhe sach me zarurat hai. Isliye main thoda kam message karne laga, kyunki mujhe laga ki shayad meri wajah se tum irritate ho rahi ho ya tumhe thoda time chahiye apne liye.

Mujhe pata hai tum apne phase me thodi overwhelmed thi, aur agar us time tum kisi se milna nahi chahti thi toh that's totally okay. Main bas yahi try kar raha tha ki hum milke calmly baat kar lein aur situation samajh lein.

Main maanta hoon ki mere efforts tumhe shayad kam lage hon, but meri intention kabhi pressure dene ki nahi thi. Bas yahi tha ki hum baith ke normal tareeke se baat karein, na ki chats pe misunderstand ho.

Agar maine tumhe kahi bhi hurt kiya ho ya uncomfortable feel karaya ho, I'm really sorry for that. That was never my intention.
